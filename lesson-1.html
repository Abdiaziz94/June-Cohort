Brownie recipe

This is is the recipe for our famous chocolate brownies,
we have kept this as a secret for far too long and have finally decided to share this with the world.

Ingredeints

  • 3 Sticks (340 g) salted butter
  • 1 pound of bittersweet or semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • Half a cup of grantulated sugar
  • 1 cup of light brown sugar
  • 5 Large eggs
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/3 cups of all purpose flour
  • Half a tablespoon of baking powder
  • Half a teaspoon of salted
  • 1 to 2 cups (170 to 320 g) semisweet chocolate chips

    • Instructions

    1. Prereheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a large rimmed baking sheet.
    2. In a large microwave-safe bowl, combine the butter and chocolate.
      Microwave in 1-minute intervals, stirring in between, until the mixture is melted and smooth
      (don't overheart, it should be slightly warm). Add the sugar and stir until combined.
    3. Whisk in the eggs and vanilla, and mix until large bubbles pop at the surface.
      It will go from looking slightly curled to really glossy and thick so keep stirring until you see the change.
      In a separate smaller bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
      Add the dry ingredients to the batter and mix once or twice.
      With dry streaks remaining, add the chocolatechip chips and mix until well-combined.
    4. Spread the batter evenly in the prepared pan.
    5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until edges are set but the middle is still slightly gooey.
      If you want extra dense, fudgy brownies, halfway through the baking time,
      carefully lift up the baking pan and let it drop on the oven rack to reselase any air bubbles (this is optional).
      let the brownies cool completely in the pan, and afer that you are all done.
